MICROSOFT OUTLOOK EXPRESS
=========================

Outlook Express Mail, elektronik posta ve haber okuyucu tabanlý bir 
Internet standartýdýr.
 
Outlook Express ile elektronik posta okumak için, SMTP ve POP3 veya

kullanmadýđýndan emin deđilseniz, yöneticinize veya Internet hizmet
sađlayýcýnýza baţvurarak SMTP/POP3 veya IMAP posta istemcilerini

Usenet gibi NNTP tabanlý haber sunucularýný kullanan ilan tahtalarý

Note to French users 
-------------------- 
In order to comply with the French laws on the use of encryption 
material, S/MIME support in Outlook Express has been disabled when 
used in France. However, SSL 2.0 and SSL 3.0 support is authorized, 
and hence left enabled.
